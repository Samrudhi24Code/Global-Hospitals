How to Fill the Hospital Submission Form

Welcome! To ensure we have the most accurate information about your hospital, please carefully follow these instructions when filling out the Hospital Submission Form.

Form Sections

  • Hospital Name: Enter the full official name of the hospital as recognized in official records.
  • Doctors Information: For each doctor, please provide:
    • Doctor's Name: Full name of the doctor.
    • Specialization: The area of expertise (e.g., Cardiology, Neurology).
    • Qualification: Highest degree or certification of the doctor.
    You can add additional doctors by clicking the "Add Another Doctor" button.
  • Specializations: List the specializations available in your hospital (e.g., Cardiology, Dermatology). Use commas to separate them.
  • Facilities: Describe the facilities offered (e.g., 24/7 Emergency, ICU, Pharmacy). This will help patients understand the resources available.
  • Hospital Image: Upload an image of the hospital building or logo. Ensure the file is in a supported format (JPEG, PNG).
  • Address: Provide the full address, including street name, city, and postal code for easy location.
  • Contact Information: Enter a phone number or email for inquiries. Ensure the contact information is valid and monitored.
  • Operating Hours: Specify the hours the hospital operates (e.g., 9 AM - 5 PM). This will help patients know when to visit.

Once you have gathered the necessary information, click the link below to go to the Hospital Submission Form.
